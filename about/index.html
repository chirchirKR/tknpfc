About Kericho Poly FC

Club Overview

Kericho Poly Fc also known as Kepo Fc is an institution football club based in Kericho County, Kenya. The club competes at the highest level of Kenyan football and represents the institution with pride, discipline, and competitive spirit.

Our Mission

To develop talented footballers, promote sportsmanship, and inspire the community through competitive and entertaining football.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading institutional football clubs in Kenya and a strong force in East African football.

Core Values

  • Discipline
  • Teamwork
  • Integrity
  • Community Development
  • Excellence

Home Ground

Kericho Poly Fc plays its home friendlies at 181 Camp Nou Sotik Stadium.
